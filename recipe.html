Ugali n Nyama Choma

Origin

Ugali and Nyama choma is a Kenyan delicacy that consists of grilled meat and ugali. Ugali is a cake or dough that is prepared from corn flour

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount/Quantity
Water 2 cups
Meat 1/2 kg
Corn flour 2 cups
Salt 1/2 table spoon
Garlic Pinch of Garlic

Step #1

Nyama Choma

  • Cut through the meat to get thin chunks
  • Rub garlic and salt on the meat
  • Put the meat on a heated grill
  • Occassionally turn the meat untill it becomes crunchy and brown

Step #1

Ugali

  • Boil the water
  • Add small amounts of flour and stir until you get a thick mixture
  • Let it cook under medium heat
  • Occassionally turn and mix the paste
  • Serve while hot with hot nyama choma
